在 Bash shell 脚本中传播所有参数

Arya

2023-02-17 04:54

我正在编写一个非常简单的脚本来调用另一个脚本，我需要将参数从我当前的脚本传播到我正在执行的脚本。

例如，我的脚本名称是foo.shand calls bar.sh。

foo.sh:

bar $1 $2 $3 $4

如果不明确指定每个参数，我该怎么做？



解答

如果你真的希望你的参数以相同的方式传递，请使用"$@"instead of plain 。$@

观察：

$ cat no_quotes.sh
#!/bin/bash
echo_args.sh $@

$ cat quotes.sh
#!/bin/bash
echo_args.sh "$@"

$ cat echo_args.sh
#!/bin/bash
echo Received: $1
echo Received: $2
echo Received: $3
echo Received: $4

$ ./no_quotes.sh first second
Received: first
Received: second
Received:
Received:

$ ./no_quotes.sh "one quoted arg"
Received: one
Received: quoted
Received: arg
Received:

$ ./quotes.sh first second
Received: first
Received: second
Received:
Received:

$ ./quotes.sh "one quoted arg"
Received: one quoted arg
Received:
Received:
Received:


更多建议

对于 bash 和其他类似 Bourne 的 shell：

bar "$@"
